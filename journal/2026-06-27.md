# 2026-06-27

## Decisions

- Focus the research on AI-native organizations.
- Use Shopify as the first case study.
- Build an evidence base rather than a collection of AI news and opinions.
- Research Microsoft next.

## Initial hypothesis

> AI shifts bottlenecks from coding to organizational systems.

The expected constraints include prioritization, context, coordination, decision-making, governance, review capacity, and leadership.

## Research direction

The project should move through a consistent sequence:

```text
Evidence → Patterns → Principles → Operating Model → Framework
```

Each company case study should distinguish between:

1. Leadership statements
2. Formal policy or process changes
3. Structural changes
4. Evidence of actual behavior
5. Measurable outcomes
6. Our interpretation

## Next research target: Microsoft

Primary question:

> What organizational changes has Microsoft made because of AI?

Areas to investigate:

- Software-development workflows
- Planning and budgeting
- Hiring and workforce composition
- Manager expectations and performance measures
- Use of internal agents
- Team structure and span of control
- Decision-making and governance
- Satya Nadella's description of a new operating model

The research should avoid treating product announcements or generic Copilot adoption as evidence of organizational transformation.
